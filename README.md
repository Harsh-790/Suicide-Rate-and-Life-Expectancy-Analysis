#  Suicide Rate, Happiness, and Life Expectancy Analysis

This repository contains a **Data Analytics & Visualization** project that explores the relationships between global suicide rates and various socio-economic/demographic indicators such as Happiness Scores, GDP per capita, Fertility Rates, and Life Expectancy for men and women.

---

##  Dataset Details
The project merges and analyzes data from two primary sources:
1. `Life expectancy.csv` - Contains country-wise data on life expectancy (men/women), happiness scores, and fertility rates.
2. `Suicide Rate.csv` - Contains country-wise suicide rates and GDP per capita.

---

##  Features & Variables Analyzed
- **Happiness Score:** Visualizing how mental well-being scores relate to suicide rates.
- **GDP per capita:** Analyzing the impact of economic status on suicide rates.
- **Fertility Rate:** Understanding population growth metrics against suicide data.
- **Life Expectancy (Men & Women):** Correlating health and longevity with suicide trends across genders.

---

##  Visualizations Included
The notebook utilizes `matplotlib` to generate scatter plots for bivariate analysis:
- Happiness Score vs. Suicide Rate
- GDP per capita vs. Suicide Rate
- Fertility Rate vs. Suicide Rate
- Life Expectancy (Men) vs. Suicide Rate
- Life Expectancy (Women) vs. Suicide Rate
